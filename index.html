The maker

Painting from
the inside out

I'm Sostika — a self-taught painter and maker based in Nepal, the Netherlands. I've been painting since I could hold a brush, but it was only after moving into my own studio space that I found the courage to call it a practice.

My work lives in the space between abstraction and the everyday — the golden hour light through kitchen windows, the weight of a rainy afternoon, the silence that follows a walk in the woods. I use a lot of earth tones and organic textures, letting the canvas breathe.

Alongside the paintings, I make small handmade objects: woven pieces, hand-thrown ceramics, and paper goods — things that carry the same unhurried attention into ordinary life.

I take on a small number of commissions each year. If you'd like a piece made for a specific space or occasion, I'd love to hear from you.
